4. The three elements are mental models, design models, and mapping. Mental models are meant to be thought out representation of the system, and is based on their memory of other systems. Design models are meant to be an model in which a programmer would use to create a system that would closely fit the user's mental model. Mapping is a process of combining both the mental model and designer model and attempts to have them combine together.
