1. What is a journey map (in your own words)!
Journey maps are made to visualize in big picture all parts of the system. This includes the actors, scenarios and expectations, and phases of the systems (life cycle).

2. How is a journey map related to user personas and scenarios?
Journey maps encompass the personas, and scenarios and try to attribute them to a real application.

3. Why are journey maps recommended?
Journey maps are recommended for the building of a system. This is due to common failure points in the cycle, as many times projects are abandoned.

4. What are 'friction points'?
Friction points are areas of problem. This is typically where a designer would attempt to mend any points of failure or frustration.

5. Your honest opinion: do you feel maps are a useful tool for a development team creating a new app?
I think in a corporate scenario this is all but required however for other projects potentially self projects I believe it could help but is not all neccessary.
